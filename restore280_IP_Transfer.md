# INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY TRANSFER AND CHARITABLE CONTRIBUTION AGREEMENT

This Intellectual Property Transfer and Charitable Contribution Agreement (this "Agreement") is entered into as of June 23, 2026, by and between:

**Transferors:** BY THE LINDEMANS LLC, an Arizona limited liability company ("LLC"), acting by and through its members Enoch Lindeman and Emalyn Marian Lindeman; and

**Transferee:** restore280 Institute, a Delaware nonprofit corporation ("restore280").

---

## 1. Background

The LLC holds a GitHub account and associated repositories, along with other software assets developed under the LLC. The LLC's members wish to make a charitable contribution of the GitHub account, all repositories hosted on it, and all other software owned by the LLC to restore280, in furtherance of restore280's exempt purposes under Section 501(c)(3) of the Internal Revenue Code.

---

## 2. Transferred Assets

The LLC hereby assigns, transfers, and conveys to restore280 all right, title, and interest in and to the following assets (collectively, the "Transferred Assets"):

**(a) GitHub Account.** The GitHub account currently operating under the handle `by-The-Lindemans`, including the account itself, all account settings, and all access credentials.

**(b) All Repositories.** All repositories currently hosted on that GitHub account, including all code, scripts, automation workflows, documentation, draft documents, configuration files, commit history, issues, pull requests, and other content within each repository, as they exist as of the effective date of this Agreement.

**(c) Other Software.** All other software, code, scripts, and related documentation developed under or owned by the LLC that is not yet hosted in the GitHub account described above.

The Transferred Assets include all intellectual property rights in the foregoing, including copyrights, trade secrets, and any other proprietary rights, to the extent held by the LLC.

---

## 3. Excluded Assets

The following assets are expressly excluded from this transfer and remain with the LLC:

(a) all social media accounts operated by the LLC;

(b) all retail operations, inventory, and associated assets;

(c) all domain names associated with retail or social media operations;

(d) all other non-software assets of the LLC not described in Section 2.

---

## 4. Open-Source Licensed Content

To the extent any Transferred Asset was previously released under an open-source license (including Creative Commons CC0 1.0, Mozilla Public License 2.0, or similar), the LLC's interest in that asset passes to restore280 subject to and consistent with the terms of the applicable license. This transfer does not affect the rights of third parties under any open-source license previously granted.

---

## 5. Charitable Contribution

This transfer is made as an in-kind charitable contribution. No consideration is paid or received by the LLC or its members. The deductibility of this contribution for federal income tax purposes depends on restore280's tax-exempt status under Section 501(c)(3), which is pending as of the date of this Agreement. The parties intend this transfer to be a charitable contribution effective as of the date restore280 is recognized as tax-exempt by the Internal Revenue Service, retroactive to its date of incorporation where permitted by applicable law.

---

## 6. Effective Date

This Agreement is effective as of June 23, 2026, the date on which the Certificate of Incorporation of restore280 Institute becomes effective under Delaware law, which is the earliest date on which restore280 Institute exists as a legal entity capable of receiving this transfer. Execution and delivery of this Agreement may occur after that date; the transfer of the Transferred Assets is deemed to have occurred as of June 23, 2026 upon such execution and delivery.

---

## 7. Electronic Signature and Execution

This Agreement may be executed by physical signature, by electronic signature transmitted by email or electronic signature platform, or by GitHub pull request approval on the pull request introducing this Agreement into the repository currently located at `https://github.com/by-The-Lindemans/governance`.

For purposes of this Agreement, a GitHub pull request approval submitted by a party whose GitHub account is identified in the signature block below constitutes a valid electronic signature by that party, with the same legal effect as a handwritten signature. This Agreement is fully executed when all three signatories identified below have approved the pull request or provided signatures by another permitted method.

The LLC and its members represent and warrant that:

(a) the LLC is the owner of the Transferred Assets, free and clear of any liens, encumbrances, or third-party claims to the extent of the LLC's interest;

(b) the members have unanimous authority to authorize this transfer under Arizona law and the LLC's governing documents;

(c) this transfer does not violate any agreement to which the LLC is a party; and

(d) to the members' knowledge, the Transferred Assets do not infringe any third-party intellectual property rights.
