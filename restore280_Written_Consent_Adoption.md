## IX. Tax and Government Filings

**RESOLVED,** that the Executive Director is authorized and directed to apply for an Employer Identification Number (EIN) from the Internal Revenue Service on behalf of the Corporation.

**RESOLVED FURTHER,** that the Executive Director is authorized to file IRS Form 1023-EZ or Form 1023, as applicable, to apply for recognition of the Corporation's tax-exempt status under Section 501(c)(3) of the Internal Revenue Code, and to execute and deliver all documents and take all actions necessary or advisable in connection with that application.

**RESOLVED FURTHER,** that the Board hereby authorizes the filing of IRS Form 5768 to elect the expenditure test for lobbying under Section 501(h) of the Internal Revenue Code, effective for the Corporation's first taxable year. The Executive Director is authorized to execute and file Form 5768.

**RESOLVED FURTHER,** that the Executive Director is authorized to file an Application for Authority with the New York Department of State and to take all steps necessary to qualify the Corporation to conduct activities in New York, including obtaining any required consents from the New York State Commissioner of Education with respect to the Corporation's name.

**RESOLVED FURTHER,** that the Executive Director is authorized to file all initial registrations required by the New York Attorney General's Charities Bureau and to take all steps necessary to maintain the Corporation's good standing in any state in which it conducts activities.

---

## X. Electronic Signatures and GitHub Pull Request Approvals

**RESOLVED,** that for purposes of all Board actions taken pursuant to Article VII of the Bylaws, a GitHub pull request approval submitted in the restore280 Institute governance repository by a director whose GitHub account is listed in the `.github/voters.yml` file of that repository constitutes a valid written consent and electronic signature by that director, with the same legal force and effect as a handwritten signature on a paper consent document.

**RESOLVED FURTHER,** that the timestamp and username recorded by GitHub for each pull request approval shall constitute the date and identity of the director's consent for purposes of Section 7.6 of the Bylaws, and the merged pull request together with its review history shall constitute the written record of Board action required by Section 7.6.

**RESOLVED FURTHER,** that this electronic signature mechanism applies to all Board actions under Article VII, including routine consents, special actions requiring a two-thirds threshold, and document authentication under Section 3.11, provided the applicable approval thresholds are satisfied.

**RESOLVED FURTHER,** that the Executive Director is authorized to maintain the `.github/voters.yml` file in the governance repository as the authoritative voter list, subject to updates approved by Board written consent whenever directors join or leave the Board.

---
